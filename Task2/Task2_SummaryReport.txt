A summary report highlighting trends, patterns, and anomalies

While doing the data cleaning-

Missing values were removed using mean (numerical) and placeholder.
Duplicate records were removed to ensure data accuracy.
Outliers in the Sales column were removed using the IQR method.

Correlation Findings

Sales and Profit show a positive correlation.
Discount and Profit show a negative correlation.
Quantity has a mild positive relationship with Sales

Trends and Patterns

Certain regions contribute significantly more to the total sales.
Some product categories generate high sales but low profit.
Extreme profit values indicate potential data anomalies or special cases.
